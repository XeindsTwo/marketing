Work process

  • Planning & Preparation

    We start by organizing the cooperation and assigning a project team to you. Together, we create a list of necessary requirements to achieve the goals and key performance indicators (KPIs) of your ad campaign.

  • Influencer Search

    At this stage, we search for influencers that match your criteria. We negotiate with them to secure the best terms for promoting your product.

  • Approval

    You select the preferred influencers to work with, confirm the terms of reference and publication deadlines, and approve draft ad videos.

  • Contracts & Publication

    We handle all legal paperwork with the selected influencers, ensuring the campaign launches on time and adheres to the approved plan.

  • Reports & Payments

    For convenience and security, we use a single payment account and offer a secure payment option. We are regulated by the state and ensure compliance with tax obligations.
